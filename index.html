MUSE Lab Mission

The Moving Intelligent User-Centric Software Engineering (MUSE) Lab at Queen's University is dedicated to advancing the field of software engineering by integrating empirical research with human-computer interaction. Our mission is to develop innovative approaches and tools that enhance the sustainability, inclusivity, and productivity of complex socio-technical ecosystems, with a particular focus on open-source software communities. By leveraging data-driven insights and machine learning techniques, we aim to empower developers and organizations to create software systems that are robust, user-centric, and adaptable to evolving requirements.

Research Directions

  • Empirical Software Engineering
  • Open-Source Software (OSS)
  • Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion (EDI)
  • Machine Learning for Software Engineering
  • Developer Productivity
